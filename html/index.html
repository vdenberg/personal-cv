Experience

Research Scientist

Leibniz ScienceCampus ‘Empirical Linguistics and Computational Language Modeling’

I started out in 2016 working on the effect of noise on Deep Learning approaches to Sentiment Analysis. Since then I have focused on implicit sentiment and entity framing. I constructed the Twitter Titling Corpus to examine the relationship between naming and stance in tweets about politicans. I then finished a German-language counterpart to this corpus and expanding my research to the portrayal of politicians in newspaper data. Now I am working on detection of Informational Entity Framing in news using various context integration methods. I've used neutral networks models (LSTM, Transformers) and libraries (Keras, PyTorch), and done data processing and analysis with Python, Pandas and Scikit-learn.
